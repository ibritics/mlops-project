# Gemini CLI Instructions for mlops-project

This document outlines the conventions and best practices for the Gemini CLI when interacting with the `mlops-project` repository. Adhering to these guidelines will ensure consistency, maintainability, and efficiency within the project.

## General Guidelines

*   **Contextual Understanding:** Before making any changes, strive to understand the surrounding code, tests, and configuration files to ensure your modifications integrate naturally and idiomatically.
*   **Proactive Clarification:** If a task or instruction is unclear or ambiguous, always ask for clarification from the user.
*   **Clarify When Unsure:** Whenever uncertain about any aspect of a task or instruction, always ask the user for clarification before proceeding.
